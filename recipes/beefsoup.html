Beef Soup

Description

A warm and delicious beef stew is sure to keep you warm during the cold days.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Corn on the cob
  • Potatoes
  • zucchini
  • Chayote (green squatch)
  • Green bell peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Cut your vegetables and beef into preferred size
  2. In a pot add a bit of oil along with chopped bell peppers, tomatoes, garlic and saute
  3. Add the beef and begin to condiment with mustard, ketchup, salt, cumming and your favorite beef flavor
  4. Add Water along with your cut vegetables and let the it come a boil
  5. Let it cook for 1 hour and season stew to taste
  6. Cook rice
  7. Serve with a side of lemons and rice
  8. Enjoy!
