Lasagna

Description

This beautiful lasagna recipe combines a perfect mix of pasta with a rich sauce and meat base. Top it off with a layer of cheese and serve it with a glass of wine and have yourself a great evening.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. ground beef.
  • 2 cups of mozzarella cheese.
  • 6 lasagna noodeles.
  • 1/4 hot water.
  • 4 cups of tomato-basil pasta sauce.
  • ricotta cheese.

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water with salt, and add the noodles for 10 minutes.
  2. Cook beef on medium heat and constntly stir until meat is cook througly.
  3. Cook pasta at low heat, and add beef when tha pasta begins to steam.
  4. In a baking dish begin to layer. first add a noodle, then the sauce with the beef, finally add the cheese. Repeat this step until necessary.
  5. Heat at 375 degrees for 45 minutes; let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy!
