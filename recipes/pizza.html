Pizza

Description

A classic pepperoni pizza with cheese and a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces of dough.
  • 12 ounces of mozzarella cheese.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Pot with hot water.
  • Black Pepper condiment.
  • Salt.
  • Granulated garlic.
  • Granulated onion.
  • Your favourite pepperoni.

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough a night before.
  2. Spread the dough.
  3. Boil a pot of water with tomatoes for 10 minutes.
  4. Bring tomatoes to a cool and begin to peel the skin and then press the tomatoes to get a consistent sauce.
  5. Add the condiments to the tomato sauce.
  6. Spread the sauce on the dough that we have prepared.
  7. Add mozzarella evenly through the pizza.
  8. Add pepperoni.
  9. Heat oven to 375 degrees.
  10. Place pizza on a baking sheet and cook for 45 minutes.
  11. Enjoy!
